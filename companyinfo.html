Company Info

HODA ENGINEERING are turnkey Electrical Contractors in the field of Electrical.
With the vast experience gathered during execution of various projects, we are quite conversant with both Geographical conditions & Peculiar requirements of various regions of India and we are looking forward to expand the horizons to improve on our strengths.
HODA ENGINEERING. is a project based organization that follows world class standards of working, offering its customers agility, efficiency and complete value for money with a personal touch. The company executes turnkey projects using state of the art tools and project management techniques in accordance with Indian and international standards. Each employee at HODA ENGINEERING is handpicked and trained to go that extra mile and set highest norms of customer satisfaction. HODA ENGINEERING. executes a spectrum of EPC projects covering the following areas:


Electrical design & engineering
  • Design and engineering of high/low voltage electrical distribution systems
  • Design and preparation of cabling layouts
  • Coordinated layout drawings of power, control, wiring schematics, earthing for building.
  • Design and installation of data and communication wiring layout.

Electrical Contracting
  • Lighting (Point Wiring, Light Fixtures, Office/Industrial)
  • Coordinated layout drawings of power, control, wiring schematics, earthing for building.
  • Power Distribution - Cable Trays, Cabling, Termination.
  • Floor Trunking Systems
  • Earthing in Copper, G.I., Cast Iron, M. S.
  • DG Sets upto 1000 KVA
  • Low Voltage Services – Telephone system, P.A./Music System, CCTV, MATV, Nurses Call, Intruder Alarm, Access Control and other services
  • Associated Steel Fabrication.
  • Structured Cabling (Networking) - Cat 5, 5E & 6 cabling along with other requirements.
  • Fire Detection & Alarm Systems (Sensors, Panels, Wiring, Integration)
